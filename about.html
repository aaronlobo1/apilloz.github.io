About Apilloz

The concept for Apilloz was conceived a decade ago in a small town in Wyoming. My sister created it out of need. Three years ago one of her friend mentioned about the difficulties she had to endure during her pregnancy. That was the aha moment that led us to pursue this project and manufacture a few prototypes for testing. We instantly knew we were upto something big when we took our sketches to manufacture or prototype in Utah. So we applied for an utility as well as a design patent. In the last two years, we got a few prototypes made and have tested them with a focus group to come up with a fully functioning product. We hope you will love our product just like the way we loved creating it.


Our Mission

We believe all life has a purpose that is the sole reason why we exist.
In order to make this world a better place we plan on giving away a part of our proceeds to not just one but two causes that we hold dearly to our heart. First, Advocating for mental illness and giving back to the mental health and wellness community and the second, childhood obesity.
